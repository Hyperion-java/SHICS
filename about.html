About Sacred Harvester

Learn more about our church, school, and our mission to serve God and community.

Our Story

Sacred Harvester was founded to glorify God through worship, education, and community service. Our church and school work hand-in-hand to provide spiritual guidance, academic excellence, and personal development for all ages.

We are committed to nurturing faith, integrity, and a sense of purpose in every individual who joins our community.

Our Vision

To be a vibrant, faith-driven community that educates, empowers, and transforms lives for God's glory.

Our Values

  • Faith: Rooted in God’s Word and guidance.
  • Excellence: Pursuing the highest standards in worship and education.
  • Community: Supporting one another in love and service.
  • Integrity: Upholding honesty, respect, and accountability.

Leadership Team

Our church and school are guided by a passionate leadership team dedicated to spiritual growth, education, and community engagement.
